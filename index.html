Who We

Are

AIIHE became a registered higher education provider under the Tertiary Education Quality and Standards Agency Act 2011 (the TEQSA Act) in 2021 and is accredited to provide the Bachelor of Business (Marketing) and the Bachelor of Business (Information Systems). We are also registered with the Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students (CRICOS), which permits us to deliver education to students studying in Australia on student visas.

Our commitment is to provide quality education that equips students with the knowledge and skills to succeed in their careers, become community leaders, and engage in lifelong learning.
